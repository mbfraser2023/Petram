About Us

Petram is a mineral exploration company with over 20 years of experience in the industry. We specialize in identifying and developing new mineral deposits, using the latest technology and industry best practices to deliver high-quality results for our clients.

Our team of experienced geologists, engineers, and mining professionals are committed to delivering the highest standards of safety, environmental stewardship, and community engagement. We believe in working collaboratively with our clients and stakeholders to ensure that our exploration projects are sustainable, responsible, and respectful of local values and traditions.
